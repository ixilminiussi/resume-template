Academic Experience
A Fully Implicit Solver for LEMs (master's thesis)
EPFL CHANGE lab | Fall 2025
Developed a fully-implicit numerical solver for a transport-limited Landscape Evolution Model, a nonlinear system of PDEs coupling surface elevation and water dynamics. Explored alternative water velocity formulations to address ill-posedness and implemented adaptive time-stepping, regularization, and Schur-complement preconditioning to improve Newton solver convergence. Assessed solver performance on steady-state 1D and 2D landscapes.

Parallelization of Nyström Algorithm
EPFL (HPC class) | Fall 2024
Implemented in parallel the randomized Nyström algorithm to compute rank-k approximations of positive semi-definite matrices. Investigated the numerical stability and parallel performance using 3 datasets and 2 sketching matrices (Gaussian and SHRT).
